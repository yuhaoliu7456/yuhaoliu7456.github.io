Yuhao Liu      

I'm Yuhao, a Ph.D. candidate at City University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Rynson W.H. Lau. Before that, I received my M.S degree from Dalian University of Technology in 2022, supervised by Prof. Xin Yang and and B.Eng. degree from Zheng Zhou University in 2019, supervised by Prof. Bo Zhang. Previously, I worked on object segmentation (e.g., image matting and referring image segmentation). Currently, my research interests focus on multi-modal vision and image editing. I'm open to collaborations and discussions, so feel free to drop me an email if you're interested.
